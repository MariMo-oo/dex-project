About This DEX

Constant-product AMM implementing x × y = k with a 0.3% swap fee.

Liquidity providers deposit TKA + TKB in the current ratio and receive LP Tokens proportional to their share. The LP Token contract enforces that only the DEX can mint or burn shares.

Two independent pools (DEX1, DEX2) enable arbitrage between different reserve ratios.
